Overview

The ride from Victoria out to Port Renfrew is very popular. Once you're past Shirley, the road opens up and allows for a lot more speed, a lot less traffic, and some really fun twisties. The route features well-maintained pavement with ocean views to the south and forested areas to the north.

Jordan River is a common stopping point for watching surfers, and the Cold Shoulder Cafe makes a great rest stop. Botanical Beach near Port Renfrew is absolutely worth the short detour â€” a spectacular rocky shoreline with tide pools carved by the sea over millennia.
