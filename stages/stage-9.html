Overview

Stage 9 is widely considered the best stage of the entire Vancouver Island Grand Loop. A fun bridge crossing launches the stage out of Woss, followed by challenging middle-section roads, and spectacular cliff-side tracks running alongside Atluck Lake with a mountain rising on one side and the lake stretching far below.

While the total distance is not extreme, the middle section can be quite challenging with larger fist-sized gravel that demands full attention.
