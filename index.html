Vancouver Island Grand Loop

The ultimate overland ADV route around the island

The VIGL stitches together the best roads Vancouver Island has to offer — scenic coastal highways, logging roads, hidden gems, and challenging backcountry gravel. Preference has been given to avoid the main highways in favour of back roads featuring scenic vistas and challenging terrain.

Download the full GPX file for your GPS device. Pair with the interactive Google Map and the Backroads Mapbook for comprehensive navigation. Individual stage files are also available in KMZ format for Google Earth.

Bike Recommendations

An adventure motorcycle is highly recommended. Many of the roads are quite rough and will be stressful on your suspension. Half the route is dirt and gravel — street bikes will struggle on the northern stages.

Safety & Gear

Carry a personal locator beacon (ResQLink or SPOT GPS), rain gear, and bear spray. Cell coverage is unreliable to nonexistent on the western side. Northern stages have active bear, cougar, and wolf populations.

⚠ Ride Responsibly

VIGL maps and stage descriptions are intended as an overview of the route rather than turn-by-turn instructions. In addition to Google Maps, it's recommended to have an accurate GPS, a copy of the Backroads Mapbook, a personal locator beacon and to know how to use them. Conditions on Vancouver Island can shift from sun to downpour in as little as 10 minutes and may render parts of the route impassable. Please take care and be ready to turn back if the situation becomes unsafe.
